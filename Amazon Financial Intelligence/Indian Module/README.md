# Indian Market Expansion Module

This module is planned for future expansion of the Financial Intelligence Platform.

The objective is to compare Amazon’s financial and operational performance with leading Indian ecommerce and technology companies such as:
- Flipkart
- Reliance Retail
- Tata Digital
- Nykaa
- Zomato
- Swiggy

Planned analytics capabilities:
- Comparative revenue analysis
- Profitability benchmarking
- Market growth comparison
- Regional ecommerce insights
- Forecasting and trend analytics

This extension is intended to evolve the project from a single-company analytics dashboard into a multi-company financial intelligence platform.
